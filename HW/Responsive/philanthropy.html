Zeta Tau Alpha has been one of four partners in the fight against Breast Cancer since 1992. Zeta partners with The American Cancer Society for surviver support, The NFL for awareness, and Bright Pink for education.

Think Pink

Every year since 2004 the chapters of Zeta Tau Alpha host a week of awareness and education known as Think Pink Week. The members of ZTA distibute pink ribbons for awareness and educate their community of the effects of Breast Cancer as well as the statistics of the different ways you may be affected.Over 19 million ribbons have been distributed by ZTA.
