    Lima and Cusco Peru

    Lima is home to one of the most amazing fallen civilizations the city of Machu Picchu. It was home to an Inca tribe in the 1500's high in the Andes mountains and is one of the most beautiful sights in South America. Besides Macchu Picchu, Lima, Peru is a beautiful city with colorful and unqiue architecture.
